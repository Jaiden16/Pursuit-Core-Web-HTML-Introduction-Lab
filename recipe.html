The Perfect Sandwich

Description

This bacon cheeseburger made from the finest qualities will make your mouth water and friends happy

Ingredients

  • Hamburger Bun
  • 3 Slices of bacon
  • 1lb of ground beef
  • 1 leaf of lettuc
  • 1 tomato slice

Directions

  1. Preheat pan and cook beef until desired
  2. Cook bacon until desired
  3. Add Toppings to Hamburger Bun
  4. Place cooked beef patty on bun with bacon slices on last
  5. Enjoy!
